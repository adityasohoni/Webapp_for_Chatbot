NITK ChatBot

What is it?

The NITK ChatBot is a Virtual Assistant to guide you through NITK and to help you find the various facilities available in the campus

How does it work ?

You can ask the bot about various facilities available in and around NITK. You can ask about food, snacks, transcripts, departments and much more.
